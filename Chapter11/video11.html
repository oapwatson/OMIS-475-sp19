Hacksaw Ridge









So, one of my favorite movies of all time is probably Hacksaw Ridge. It’s the story of Pfc Desmond Doss who won the Congressional Medal of Honor during WW2. He had a hard early life which led to his decision to be a conscientious objector to WW2 based on religious grounds and refused to fire a weapon during the war. Because of this, he was made a medic and saved many people behind enemy lines. It’s a fantastic movie that everyone should watch.
