Even though I have lived in Illinois my entire life, I had only been to Chicago a few times before I met my girlfriend. We started going there together many times just to explore and take in the sights. We have gone to almost all of the museums and we just went to the Chicago Auto Show as well! We plan on exploring many more things in the city! The truck in the pic to the right is a Chevy Silverado completely made out of Legos we saw at the auto show!





    Louisville

    My junior year of college I had the ability to go to Louisville to campaign for a Senater who was running for re-election. Becuase of this, I did get to do a lot of exploring of the city in a short period of time. Unfortunately I didn't take many pictures while I was there but I will never forget my time spent at this city.




      Washington DC

      Over the summer I spent a week in DC between my freshman and sophomore year. I heard from many speakers and learned a lot from these speakers. I didn't get to do much sight seeing while I was in the city as I only went out once or twice, but I did still manage to have a lot of fun while I was there and will definitely be returning in the future!
